Research Interests

I am currently exploring the optimization of machine learning in the context of robotics and computer vision, as well as multi-modal perception. My goal is to integrate deep learning and optimization techniques into robots and AI systems to enhance their robustness and capability to perceive and interpret their surroundings.

During my undergraduate studies, I have explored several topics, including:

  • Generative AI and its Applications: How to use convolutional neural networks to model and generate data effectively.
  • Medical Image Enhancement and Analysis: Employing unsupervised learning to address the challenges associated with small samples in medical anomaly detection.
  • Object Detection: How to enhance the perceptual efficiency of detection models in understanding the world.
