Research Interests

Currently, my research interests are deep learning, machine learning, and their applications to computer vision. Specifically, I focus on:

  • Generative AI and its applications (e.g., unsupervised anomaly detection, image translation).
  • Medical image enhancement and analysis.
  • Multimodal Perception: effectively incorporate multiple modalities (e.g., vision, language) into AI systems to make them extract more information from the surrounding world.
